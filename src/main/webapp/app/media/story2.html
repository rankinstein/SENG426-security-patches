ACME Ltd. appoints John Gallager as President to Lead International Growth Strategy

Cupertino, CA â€“ April 20, 2016 - Jo Thomas, Founder and Chairman of ACME Ltd, is pleased to announce that John Gallager has joined the firm as President. Mr. Gallager will provide leadership to the growth and development of operational and sales capacity, with an initial focus on managing ACME's current rapid growth in the United States and other foreign markets.

Mr. Gallager has been involved with ACME since its founding, and has been a Director for several years. Most recently he has been a Partner at Lightspeed Security Inc. with corporate responsibility for the Enterprise Solutions Practice, generating over $40 million in annual sales. He joined Lightspeed, after selling Gallager Consulting Ltd., a management consultancy, to Lightspeed Inc. in 2012.

Gallager brings to ACME extensive experience as a senior manager in both the private and public sectors. He served with the Government of the State of Virginia as Chief Information Officer (CIO). Prior to service with the Virginia State Government he built a successful private software firm. He participates on the Board of several private companies.
